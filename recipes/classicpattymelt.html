Classic Patty Melt

A delicous patty melt sandwich

featuring a ground beef patty with melted cheese and caramelized onions on grilled rye bread. It combines the flavors of a burger and a grilled cheese, offering a savory, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Real bacon bits
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1/4 tsp Onion powder
  • 2 tbsp Ketchup
  • 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Sweet relish
  • 3/4 tsp Ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Sea salt
  • White sugar
  • Rye bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Provolone cheese

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine ground beef, bacon bits, onion powder, black pepper, sea salt, and sugar. Mix thoroughly and form into patties that match the size of your bread slices.
  2. Preheat a skillet over medium heat and cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your preferred level of doneness.
  3. In a small bowl, combine ketchup, mayonnaise, and sweet relish to make a tangy burger sauce.
  4. Butter one side of each slice of rye bread. On the non-buttered side of three slices, spread some of the sauce, then place a slice of cheddar cheese, a cooked patty, a slice of provolone cheese, and another slice of bread with the buttered side up.
  5. Heat a clean skillet over medium heat. Grill each sandwich until the bread is golden and crispy and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
