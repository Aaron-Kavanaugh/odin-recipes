Chicken Enchiladas

A delicous plate of Chicken Enchiladas

These Chicken Enchiladas with Sour Cream White Sauce are a perfect blend of creamy and savory flavors. Ideal for family dinners or gatherings, this dish is sure to impress with its rich taste and delightful texture.

  • 2 cups Chicken, cooked
  • 1 cup Corn, frozen kernels
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 cup Green chilies
  • 1 Onion, small
  • 1 cup Chicken broth
  • 1 tsp Chili powder
  • 1 Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin, ground
  • 8 Flour tortillas, small
  • 1 cup Monterey jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup Sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© until the onions become translucent.
  4. Mix in the green chilies, frozen corn, and cooked chicken. Season with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cook the mixture for about 5-7 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
  6. In a medium saucepan, warm the chicken broth over medium heat.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the sour cream until smooth.
  8. Add half of the grated Parmesan cheese to the sauce, stirring until the sauce is creamy and the cheese has melted.
  9. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Spoon the chicken mixture onto each flour tortilla.
  11. Roll up the tortillas and place them seam-side down in a lightly greased baking dish.
  12. Pour the sour cream white sauce over the rolled enchiladas.
  13. Sprinkle the top with Monterey Jack cheese and the remaining Parmesan cheese.
  14. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven.
  15. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and lightly golden.
  16. Let the enchiladas cool for a few minutes before serving.
  17. Garnish with additional sour cream, chopped cilantro, or sliced green onions if desired.
