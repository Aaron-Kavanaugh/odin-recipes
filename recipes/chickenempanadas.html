Chicken Empanadas

A delicous plate of Chicken Empanadas

Tender, juicy shredded chicken, onions, peppers and garlic make a hearty, savory filling for our easy chicken empanada recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs 2 cups shredded chicken breast
  • 1 Bell pepper, yellow or orange
  • 2 tbsp Cilantro
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup Onion
  • 1 tsp Onion powder
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp Chicken bouillon
  • 1 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 10 Empanada dough shells
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil, extra virgin

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© chopped onions, minced garlic, and diced bell peppers until soft.
  3. Add shredded chicken, tomato paste, chicken bouillon, garlic powder, onion powder, and kosher salt. Cook for a few minutes until well combined.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in chopped cilantro. Let the filling cool.
  5. Spoon the chicken mixture onto the center of each empanada dough shell.
  6. Fold the dough over to form a half-moon shape. Seal the edges by pressing down with a fork.
  7. Beat the egg with a tablespoon of water to make an egg wash.
  8. Brush the top of each empanada with the egg wash.
  9. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit (200 degrees Celsius).
  10. Place the empanadas on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
